Welcome! Here you can play a game in which one person will draw and other people will try to guess what that person is drawing!

Click 'play' in order to sign in and join another player in a game.

Wait until you know the other person can see your name before you pick them, or else your name will disappear from the list of available users since you will technically be in a game at that point.

If you wish to play the mobile version, you may have to switch to landscape mode or double-tap on your screen twice to be able to see the area where you can type in your guess

Currently, users are limited to one round. If you would like to play again just find the user and pick them again.

There is also a current bug that's disabling the drawing area if you switch windows, so try to avoid that.


**Mobile play is limited to a contestant only. Artist play is unavailable at the moment.**
